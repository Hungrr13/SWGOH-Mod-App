# ModForge Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-19_

ModForge ("we", "the app") is a companion utility for the mobile game
Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes. This policy explains what data the app handles
and how it handles it.

ModForge is **not** affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Electronic
Arts, Capital Games, or Lucasfilm. All Star Wars trademarks are the property
of their respective owners.

## Permissions and what we do with them

| Permission                              | Why                                                                                       | What we do with the data                                                                  |
| --------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Display over other apps (overlay)       | To show a small floating "scan" button while you are in the game.                         | Nothing. The overlay only displays a button. It does not read other apps' content.        |
| Screen capture (MediaProjection)        | To take one screenshot of the SWGOH mod inspect screen each time you tap the scan button. | The screenshot is processed entirely on your device, then discarded. It is never uploaded. |
| Foreground service                      | Android requires this so the overlay button can stay attached while the game is open.     | We post a small notification while the service runs. No data is collected.                 |
| Notifications                           | To show the foreground-service notification described above.                              | We do not send marketing or remote-content notifications.                                  |
| Internet                                | To optionally fetch your roster from a public ally-code lookup service when you ask.      | Only your ally code is sent, and only when you tap "Load". No tracking.                    |

## What ModForge collects

ModForge **does not collect, transmit, or share** any personally identifying
information. There are no analytics, no advertising identifiers tracked or
sold, no user accounts, and no cloud sync.

The following is stored **locally on your device only**, and you can clear it
at any time by uninstalling the app or using the in-app "Clear" controls:

- Your SWGOH ally code (if you choose to enter one) and the roster snapshot
  retrieved from the public ally-code lookup service.
- Saved scanner training samples (cropped icons of your own mods) used to
  improve set / shape recognition on your device.
- App preferences (e.g. light/dark theme).

## What ModForge transmits

- **Ally-code lookups:** when you enter an ally code and tap Load, that ally
  code is sent to a public roster lookup service to fetch your public roster
  data. No other personal information is sent.
- **Ads:** the app may display ads through Google AdMob. Ad serving uses
  standard AdMob signals as described in
  [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Screenshots and mod images:** never transmitted. They are processed in
  memory on your device and discarded.

## Data sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties beyond the ad network
described above.

## Children

ModForge is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
data from children.

## Your choices

- You can use the app without ever entering an ally code; in that case no
  outbound network call is made on your behalf.
- You can revoke the overlay permission, screen-capture permission, or
  notification permission at any time in Android Settings.
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data ModForge has stored.

## Security

Local data is stored in the app's private storage area, which is sandboxed
by the operating system. We do not transmit screen contents, and no remote
backup is performed by the app itself.

## Changes to this policy

If material changes are made we will update the date at the top of this page
and update the in-app About / Settings screen so you can review the change
before continuing to use the app.
